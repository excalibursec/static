about us

From a young age, my interest in technology and computers has been a driving passion. After earning my BSc in Cyber Security, I discovered a strong passion for testing systems and entered the professional field, helping businesses understand the related business risks. As I explored further into the world of cyber risks present in today's interconnected networks, I naturally extended my knowledge to assist my friends and family. I began helping them understand and apply controls to improve both their online safety and the safety of their children in our ever-changing digital world.

As a Cyber Security Expert and a proud uncle to four wonderful kids, I wholeheartedly grasp the anxieties that parents experience as they navigate the complexities of the tech and internet realm. My mission is straightforward: I aim to provide parents with the necessary tools to ensure their kids stay safe online while still enjoying the wonders of the digital age.
